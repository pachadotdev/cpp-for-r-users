# cpp-for-r-users

## Motivation

Here is a collection of my very own C++ errors and how I try to solve them.

In particular, these notes are for ECE244 (Programming Fundamentals) at the
University of Toronto.

I tend to lose focus and get distracted. Therefore, I prefer to organize my
notes to minimize the likelihood of that.

## Honest disclaimer

This blog post is a summary of what worked after hours of fails for my future
self. I hope it helps you  too.

I am a Statistician and Political Scientist, **not** a Computer Scientist!
